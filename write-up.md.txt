# Design Rationale

## Why these questions?
The questions reflect real internal dialogue at the end of a workday. Instead of ideal answers, options capture hesitation, avoidance, and trade-offs people actually experience.

## Branching logic
Branching is based on:
- Direct answers
- Accumulated signals across axes

This ensures deterministic but adaptive flow.

## Psychological grounding
- Axis 1: Rotter (Locus of Control), Dweck (Growth Mindset)
- Axis 2: Campbell (Entitlement), Organ (OCB)
- Axis 3: Maslow (Self-transcendence), Batson (Perspective-taking)

## Why fixed options?
Fixed options force clarity. The user must recognize themselves in predefined patterns, enabling consistent reflection.

## Improvements
- More granular signal weighting
- UI layer for better experience
- Additional mid-path reflections
